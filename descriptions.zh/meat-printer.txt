* *keypad* is an XBus input connected to a keypad.
* *extrude* is a simple output connected to a meat extruder.
* *valve-0* through *valve-2* are simple outputs connected to valves in the meat extruder that control whether LEAN MIX (low) or FAT MIX (high) is extruded.
* When a value is available from the *keypad*, read it and assemble the corresponding MEAT SPECIFICATION, which are found on a page in the “Supplemental Data” section of the manual:

##############

| 1 | cutlet-style meat
------------------------
| 2 | steak-style meat
------------------------
| 3 | bacon-style meat
