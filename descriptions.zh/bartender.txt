* *keypad* is an XBus input connected to a keypad.
* *vodka*, *gin*, *lemon*, *lime*, *cranberry*, and *vermouth* are simple outputs connected to mechanisms that dispense those ingredients at a rate of 0.5 oz. per time unit.
* When a value is available from the *keypad*, read it and assemble the corresponding COCKTAIL RECIPE from “The Elegant Bachelor,” which can be found in the “Supplemental Data” section of the manual:

##############

| 1 | vodka shot    | 5 | vodka martini
----------------------------------------
| 2 | lemon drop    | 6 | gimlet
----------------------------------------
| 3 | cosmopolitan  | 7 | gin martini
----------------------------------------
| 4 | cape cod      |   |
